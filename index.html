Intresting Points About Camel

  1. There are two types of camels: One humped or “dromedary” camels and two humped Bactrian camels.
  2. Camels have three sets of eyelids and two rows of eyelashes to keep sand out of their eyes.
  3. Camels have thick lips which let them forage for thorny plants other animals can't eat.

Intresting Points About Chameleon

  • Chameleons vary wildly in size.
  • They mainly change their colour in order to communicate or regulate body temperature.
  • Skin crystals help them to change colour at will.

Intresting Points About Pigeon

  1. Pigeons are incredibly complex and intelligent animals.
  2. Pigeons are renowned for their outstanding navigational abilities.
  3. Pigeons are highly sociable animals.

Intresting Points About Bear

  • Bears are extraordinarily intelligent animals.
  • Some species of Asiatic bear build nests in the trees.
  • Bears grieve deeply for others.

Intresting Points About Panda

  1. Pandas go from pink to white and black (or brown).
  2. An adult can eat 12–38 kilos of bamboo per day.
  3. Pandas are "lazy" — eating and sleeping make their day.

Intresting Points About Cicada

  • Cicadas can survive a huge fall as babies, or nymphs.
  • Females may be attracted to the sound of motors.
  • The loud whirring or buzzing sound you hear is an all-male cicada chorus.
